About Us

Company Profile

Krise International is a New Delhi-based trading company delivering globally sourced industrial solutions since 2010. Built on decades of market knowledge in polymers, moulds, sheet metal tools, and machinery, we help manufacturers reduce procurement risk and improve operational readiness.

Our Story

Founded by Mr. Rakesh and Mr. Naveen Grover, Krise International was established to bridge Indian and international manufacturers with dependable industrial supply channels. Over the years, we have built trusted relationships across regions by combining product understanding, quality discipline, and responsive client communication.

Today, our business serves customers across worldwide markets with a practical approach: understand the technical requirement, align compliant supply options, and support every stage from inquiry to dispatch.
